Fire Station Bake Sale

Sweet Treats for a Sweet Cause!

Join us for the annual Fire Station Bake Sale! Indulge in delicious homemade treats while supporting our hardworking firefighters.

  • Date: Saturday, March 15th, 2024
  • Time: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM
  • Location: Fire Station #5, Main Street

All proceeds go towards upgrading our fire safety equipment and community programs.

Delicious Goodies Await!

Here’s a sneak peek at what’s baking:

  • Freshly baked cookies, including chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, and sugar cookies.
  • Moist and flavorful cakes in various flavors like red velvet and carrot cake.
  • Homemade pies, from apple to pecan to pumpkin.
  • Specialty bread, including banana bread, zucchini bread, and more.
